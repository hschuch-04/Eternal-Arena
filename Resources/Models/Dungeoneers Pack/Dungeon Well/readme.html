Description

Every dungeon has a healing well.

This is supposed to work like the Night Elf moonwell, and works with the ability "Replenish Health and Mana", but can also be used with coded abilities: Just attach the water effect (The one used by the moon well ingame, or the coming with this model) to the sprite attachment and use the "Alternate" animations (There is three of them for 3 different levels of "water".

If the water effect is too low, try using the attachment point "Sprite".

This model includes a dungeon well, and a custom red version of the moon well effect to works as a well that only heals health.

(The normal moon well heals 4 life or 2 mana for each 2 mana spend I think).
